Evaluation Guide

📐
Layout hierarchy: Which puts the three beats (number → trend → tip) in the clearest reading order?
🤫
Quiet voice: Which feels most like a coach who doesn't need to raise their voice?
⏱️
30-second scan: Which can you absorb in a single glance without scrolling?
📱
Material 3 fit: Which will map most naturally to Flutter's Material 3 widget library?
🔄
Scalability: Which handles graceful degradation ("Based on 47% of strokes") and baseline building ("37/100 strokes") without breaking?
